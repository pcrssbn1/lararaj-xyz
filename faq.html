Who is Lara Raj?

Lara Raj is a member of KATSEYE, the six-member global girl group formed through HYBE and Geffen Records' survival competition The Debut: Dream Academy in 2023. She was selected as one of six finalists from a pool of 20 international contestants. The group officially debuted on June 28, 2024, with their mini-album 'SIS (Soft Is Strong).' Lara serves as one of the group's dancers and vocalists, bringing strong contemporary dance skills and stage presence to KATSEYE's performances. Her journey from contestant to debut artist was documented in both the competition series and the subsequent Netflix documentary 'Pop Star Academy: KATSEYE,' which provided viewers with insight into the intensive training process and the challenges of breaking into the global music industry.

How old is Lara Raj from KATSEYE?

Lara Raj was born on October 29, 2006, making her 17 years old at the time of KATSEYE's debut in June 2024 and currently 18 years old as of late 2024. She is the second-youngest member of the group, with only Yoonchae being younger (born 2007). Her age places her squarely within Generation Z, and she represents the emerging generation of artists who grew up with social media and global pop culture as constants in their lives. Despite her young age, Lara demonstrated remarkable professionalism and skill during The Debut competition, competing against contestants up to four years older. The age range within KATSEYE spans from 2002 to 2007, creating a dynamic where members bring different generational perspectives while remaining close enough in age to relate to shared experiences.

What is Lara Raj's ethnicity and background?

Lara Raj is of Indian descent and is American by nationality. She represents South Asian heritage within KATSEYE's intentionally diverse lineup, which includes members of Filipino, Swiss-Ghanaian, Venezuelan, and Korean backgrounds. Her ethnic background has significance beyond personal identity—it represents increased visibility for South Asian artists in mainstream pop music, where representation has historically been limited. Growing up in the United States with Indian heritage, Lara experienced a multicultural upbringing that influences her artistic perspective. The fusion of her cultural background with the global pop and K-pop influenced training she received creates a unique artistic foundation. HYBE and Geffen Records explicitly designed KATSEYE to reflect global diversity, recognizing that modern audiences value authentic representation and multicultural perspectives in their entertainment.

Where is Lara Raj from?

Lara Raj is from the United States, though specific details about her hometown have been kept relatively private. What is publicly known is that she trained and competed in the Los Angeles area, where The Debut: Dream Academy was primarily filmed and where KATSEYE is based. Los Angeles serves as a major hub for entertainment industry training, particularly for artists aiming for global careers. The city's diverse population and concentration of dance studios, vocal coaches, and entertainment companies made it an ideal location for her pre-debut training. KATSEYE operates primarily out of Los Angeles, which positions the group uniquely as a K-pop influenced act based in the United States rather than South Korea. This geographic positioning is part of HYBE's strategy to create a truly global group that can navigate both Western and Asian entertainment markets effectively.

When did Lara Raj debut with KATSEYE?

Lara Raj officially debuted with KATSEYE on June 28, 2024, when the group released their first mini-album 'SIS (Soft Is Strong)' and the lead single 'Touch.' The debut was accompanied by a music video that garnered over 15 million views in its first week. The debut showcase took place at the YouTube Theater in Los Angeles before an audience of over 6,000 fans. This debut came approximately seven months after the final lineup was announced in November 2023, during which time the members underwent intensive preparation including additional training, album recording, choreography refinement, and content filming. The relatively quick turnaround from lineup announcement to debut reflects modern entertainment industry practices where momentum from competition shows is leveraged immediately. Following debut, KATSEYE quickly secured high-profile promotional opportunities, including a performance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in July 2024, demonstrating the significant industry support behind the group's launch.
